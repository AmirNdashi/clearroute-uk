About This Service

The DVSA practical driving test must be booked through the official government portal, and test slots in popular areas are often limited. ClearRoute UK monitors availability, secures your preferred slot, and connects you with a DVSA-approved driving instructor in your area so your lessons align perfectly with your test date.

We also provide a test day preparation briefing — covering what the examiner looks for, common fail points, and how to manage nerves — so you walk in prepared and confident.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to have passed my theory test first?
Yes — you must have a valid theory test pass certificate before you can book a practical test. If you haven't taken your theory test yet, we can assist with that through our Theory Test Booking service.
How long does it take to get a practical test slot?
This varies by location and time of year. Popular test centres in cities can have 6–8 week waits, while quieter centres are often much sooner. We monitor availability actively to get you the earliest suitable slot.
Can I use my own car for the practical test?
Yes, you can use your own vehicle as long as it meets DVSA requirements (roadworthy, insured, with L-plates and a functioning rear-view mirror for the examiner). Most candidates use their instructor's car. We'll advise on the best option for your circumstances.
