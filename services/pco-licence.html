About This Service

The PCO (Private Hire Vehicle) licence is required to work as a private hire or taxi driver in London. The application process is managed by Transport for London (TfL) and involves multiple steps including enhanced DBS checks, medical assessments, theory tests, practical assessments, and topographical tests.

ClearRoute UK provides comprehensive guidance through the entire PCO licence process. We assess your eligibility, guide you through document preparation, help you prepare for all required tests, and ensure your application is submitted correctly to TfL. Our service is designed to take the complexity out of the process so you can focus on your new career.

Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to get a PCO licence?
The typical timeline is 15–20 days from start to finish. This depends on how quickly you can complete the required tests and assessments, and TfL processing times.
Do I need to pass all tests before applying?
You can start the application process before completing all tests, but you must pass the theory, topographical, and practical assessments before TfL will issue your licence.
What is the topographical test?
The topographical test assesses your knowledge of London's geography, including major roads, landmarks, and routes. It's a requirement for all PCO licence applicants.
