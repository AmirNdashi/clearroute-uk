About This Service

The UK theory test is administered by the DVSA (Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency) and must be passed before you can take your practical driving test. It consists of a multiple-choice section and a hazard perception test.

ClearRoute UK handles the DVSA booking process on your behalf, secures a convenient test centre slot near you, and provides access to curated study materials — including the official Highway Code, practice question banks, and hazard perception video training — so you're fully prepared before test day.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need a provisional licence before booking the theory test?
Yes — you must have a valid UK provisional driving licence before you can book a DVSA theory test. If you don't have one yet, we can also assist with the provisional licence application process.
What does the theory test consist of?
The UK theory test has two parts: a 50-question multiple choice section (you need 43/50 to pass) and a hazard perception test with 14 video clips (you need 44/75 to pass). Both must be passed in the same sitting.
How long is a theory test pass certificate valid for?
Your theory test pass certificate is valid for 2 years. You must pass your practical driving test within this period, otherwise you'll need to retake the theory test.
